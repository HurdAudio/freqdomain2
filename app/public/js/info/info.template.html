FreqDomain2


FreqDomain2 is a modular composition and sound design application built with the Web Audio API. Individual components can be chained together to construct sounding objects and realize full compositions. Users are encouraged to sonify, share and collaborate in creating sounds.

Where to start?

Mixer Settings

Create a mixer that routes your creation from the input manager to your master volume and ultimately to your speakers and ears.

Patch Editor

Build sounding objects using sonic sources and modulators. Use oscillators to modulate other oscillators, the weather in Madrid to set your dynamic compression ratio, flight times at O'Hare to set your notch filter, set stock market fluctuations to drive your envelope generator or just use census data to control the lowpass filter. FreqDomain2 allows you to mix and match different modules to realize dynamic sonic designs. New modules are being added all the time to expand the creative toolset.

Compose

Bring your patches and mixer settings into the composition editor where they can be triggered along a timeline to realize your latest masterpieces.

Be Social

Share your creation with other users or draw inspiration from others. Or just start a collaborative project with your friends and get your jam on. And be sure to check out the user hub for updates about FreqDomain2 as well as messages from the community.
