FreqDomain2


FreqDomain2 is a modular composition and sound design application built with the Web Audio API. Individual components can be chained together to construct sounding objects and realize full compositions. Users are encouraged to sonify, share and collaborate in creating sounds.



Where to start?



1
⇨

Mixer Settings

First: You will need a mixer. You define the path that an audio signal takes on its way to the speakers and ultimately to your years. This can be as simple as routing an output straight to the master volume controller or setting up an effects chain.

2
⇨

Patch Editor

Second: Let's build out some patches using sonic sources and modulators to run through your shiny new mixer. FreqDomain2 allows you to mix and match different modules to realize your sonic vision. New modules are being added all the time to expand the creative toolset.

3
⇨

Compose

Third: Now bring your collection of patches over to the composition editor where they can be triggered along a timeline to realize your masterpiece. Apply a wide range of onset, aftertouch and key-off parameters to breathe dynamicism into your sonic realization.

4

Be Social

Fourth: Don't keep your creations to yourself. Share and collaborate with other FreqDomain2 users in our social marketspace. Check out and share your mixers, patches and compositions with like-minded composers or just find inspiration for your next work.
